Choosing Your Arena: European vs. American Roulette

This is the most important strategic choice you will make at the roulette table.

  • European Roulette: Features a wheel with 37 pockets (numbers 1-36 and a single green 0). The house edge is a player-friendly 2.7%.

  • American Roulette: Features a wheel with 38 pockets (numbers 1-36, a green 0, and a green 00). This extra pocket almost doubles the house edge to 5.26%.

The lesson is simple: Always choose European Roulette when you can. It gives you a significantly better mathematical chance of winning. Never waste your precious free play bonus on a high-edge American table if a European one is available.
